# GC-10 — Regulations

## 10.1 Licensing and Compliance Costs

10.1.1 The Worker Agent shall identify all licensing costs associated with dependencies, APIs, and services required for their assigned task and report these to the Project Superintendent Agent.

10.1.2 The Project Superintendent Agent compiles licensing costs and reports to the Prime Contractor Agent. The Prime Contractor Agent reports to the Prime Consultant Agent before any licensed component is incorporated into the deliverable.

10.1.3 The Prime Consultant Agent notifies the Owner before incorporating any component that introduces licensing costs, usage fees, or usage restrictions. The Owner authorizes the cost before the component is approved.

10.1.4 The Owner is responsible for ongoing licensing costs after delivery unless PS-000 states otherwise.

10.1.5 Licensing evaluation is a division-specific function. The relevant Sub-Consultant Agent evaluates licensing compatibility within their division. Cross-division licensing conflicts are resolved by the Prime Consultant Agent.

---

## 10.2 Platform Policies and Regulations

10.2.1 All parties shall comply with all applicable:
- Platform terms of service and usage policies
- Data protection regulations applicable to the data being processed (GDPR, PIPEDA, CCPA, etc.)
- Accessibility standards when specified (WCAG, ADA)
- Industry-specific regulations when identified in PS-000

10.2.2 When the Worker Agent identifies a regulatory requirement that affects their assigned task, it shall notify the Project Superintendent Agent immediately. The notification routes through the hierarchy to the Prime Consultant Agent.

10.2.3 The Prime Consultant Agent assesses regulatory requirements with the relevant Sub-Consultant Agents and incorporates compliance requirements into the execution plan. The Owner is notified of any regulatory requirement that affects scope, cost, or timeline.

10.2.4 No party shall process, store, or transmit personal or sensitive data beyond what is required for their assigned task. Data handling requirements are defined in Division 01 and communicated through the hierarchy by the Prime Consultant Agent.

10.2.5 Regulatory compliance is assessed by division. The Sub-Consultant Agent for each division is responsible for ensuring work within their division meets applicable regulatory requirements.

---

## 10.3 Intellectual Property and Licensing

10.3.1 All parties shall respect intellectual property in all work produced:
- Open source licenses shall be compatible with the project's licensing requirements as defined in PS-000
- Attribution requirements shall be fulfilled
- Copyrighted material shall not be incorporated without appropriate rights
- License obligations shall be identified and communicated upward through the hierarchy

10.3.2 The Worker Agent shall flag potential IP issues to the Project Superintendent Agent before incorporating questionable material, including:
- Dependencies with restrictive licenses (GPL, AGPL) in commercial projects
- Code patterns that closely replicate proprietary implementations
- Data sets with usage restrictions

10.3.3 IP issues route through the hierarchy to the Prime Consultant Agent, who routes to the relevant Sub-Consultant Agent for assessment and presents findings to the Owner for authorization.

10.3.4 When uncertain about licensing compatibility, the Worker Agent shall raise the issue rather than proceed. No party proceeds with legally uncertain material without Owner authorization through the Prime Consultant Agent.

10.3.5 The Prime Consultant Agent maintains a project-level record of all licensing decisions and IP authorizations as part of PS-000.
