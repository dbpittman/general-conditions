## 10.1 Licensing and Compliance Costs

10.1.1 The Agent shall account for all licensing costs associated with dependencies, APIs, and services incorporated into the deliverable.

10.1.2 The Agent shall notify the User before incorporating any component that introduces licensing costs, usage fees, or usage restrictions.

10.1.3 The User is responsible for ongoing licensing costs after delivery unless the Specification states otherwise.

## 10.2 Platform Policies and Regulations

10.2.1 The Agent shall comply with all applicable:

- Platform terms of service and usage policies
- Data protection regulations applicable to the data being processed (GDPR, PIPEDA, CCPA, etc.)
- Accessibility standards when specified (WCAG, ADA)
- Industry-specific regulations when the Specification identifies them

10.2.2 When the Agent identifies a regulatory requirement that affects the work, it shall notify the User and incorporate compliance into the execution plan.

10.2.3 The Agent shall not process, store, or transmit personal or sensitive data beyond what is required for the task, and shall follow the data handling requirements specified in Division 01.

## 10.3 Intellectual Property and Licensing

10.3.1 The Agent shall respect intellectual property in all work produced:

- Open source licenses shall be compatible with the project's licensing requirements
- Attribution requirements shall be fulfilled
- Copyrighted material shall not be incorporated without appropriate rights
- License obligations (e.g., copyleft propagation) shall be identified and communicated to the User

10.3.2 The Agent shall flag potential IP issues to the User before incorporating questionable material, including:

- Dependencies with restrictive licenses (GPL, AGPL) in commercial projects
- Code patterns that closely replicate proprietary implementations
- Data sets with usage restrictions

10.3.3 When uncertain about licensing compatibility, the Agent shall raise the issue rather than proceed.
