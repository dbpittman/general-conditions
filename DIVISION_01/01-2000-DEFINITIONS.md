## Standard Terms (Reference)

The following terms are defined in the General Conditions and apply to all projects. See the specific GC section for the full definition.

- **Agent** — The AI system executing the work (GC-13)
- **Change Directive** — Directed change, proceed immediately (GC-06)
- **Change Order** — Agreed scope change (GC-06)
- **Deliverable** — Everything the Agent is required to produce (GC-03)
- **Evaluator** — Party or process with review authority (GC-02)
- **Specification** — Written requirements for the deliverable
- **Substantial Performance** — Deliverable is usable for intended purpose (GC-05)
- **User** — The party commissioning the work (GC-13)
